Finance

Chief Accountant

Oversee accounting operations, financial statements, and compliance

Key Responsibilities

  • Oversee day-to-day accounting operations, including general ledger, accounts payable, accounts receivable, and payroll
  • Prepare and review monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements
  • Ensure compliance with applicable accounting standards, tax laws, and statutory regulations
  • Manage budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning processes
  • Monitor cash flow, expenses, and financial performance
  • Coordinate audits and liaise with external auditors, tax consultants, and regulatory authorities
  • Maintain internal controls to safeguard company assets and prevent financial discrepancies
  • Supervise and guide accounting staff, ensuring accuracy and efficiency
  • Ensure timely filing of GST, TDS, income tax, and other statutory returns
  • Support management with financial analysis, reports, and recommendations

Qualifications & Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field (CA / CMA / CPA preferred)
  • Proven experience in accounting or finance, with leadership responsibility
  • Strong knowledge of accounting principles, taxation, and compliance
  • Proficiency in accounting software and MS Excel
  • Excellent analytical, organizational, and problem-solving skills
  • High level of integrity, accuracy, and attention to detail
  • Strong communication and team management skills

Trading

Crypto Trader

Execute cryptocurrency trades across spot, futures, and derivatives markets

Key Responsibilities

  • Execute cryptocurrency trades across spot, futures, and derivatives markets based on approved strategies
  • Monitor real-time market movements, price trends, volume, and liquidity across multiple exchanges
  • Analyze technical indicators, charts, and market data to identify profitable trading opportunities
  • Manage risk through proper position sizing, stop-loss strategies, and capital allocation
  • Track portfolio performance, profit & loss (P&L), and exposure across assets
  • Stay updated with crypto market news, regulatory updates, and macroeconomic factors affecting digital assets
  • Maintain detailed trade logs, reports, and performance summaries
  • Ensure adherence to company trading policies, risk limits, and compliance guidelines
  • Coordinate with risk management, compliance, and operations teams as required
  • Provide regular market insights, trade rationale, and performance reports to management

Qualifications & Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Business, or a related field (certifications in trading or blockchain are a plus)
  • Proven experience in cryptocurrency trading, financial markets, or related trading roles
  • Strong understanding of crypto markets, blockchain fundamentals, and digital asset ecosystems
  • Proficiency in trading platforms, charting tools, and technical analysis software
  • Solid knowledge of risk management, leverage, and portfolio diversification
  • Excellent analytical, decision-making, and problem-solving skills
  • High level of discipline, emotional control, and attention to detail
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work in fast-paced market environments

Trading

Commodity Trader

Execute commodity trades across physical and derivative markets

Key Responsibilities

  • Execute commodity trades across physical and derivative markets, including futures, options, and forwards, based on approved trading strategies
  • Monitor real-time market movements, price trends, supply–demand dynamics, volume, and liquidity across commodity exchanges
  • Analyze technical indicators, charts, and fundamental market data to identify profitable trading opportunities
  • Manage trading risk through effective position sizing, stop-loss strategies, and capital allocation
  • Track portfolio performance, profit & loss (P&L), and exposure across commodity assets
  • Stay updated on global economic indicators, geopolitical developments, weather patterns, and regulatory changes impacting commodity markets
  • Maintain accurate trade logs, reports, and performance summaries
  • Ensure adherence to company trading policies, risk limits, and regulatory compliance requirements
  • Coordinate with risk management, compliance, and operations teams to support smooth trading activities
  • Provide regular market insights, trade rationale, and performance reports to management

Qualifications & Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Business, Agriculture, or a related field (professional trading certifications are a plus)
  • Proven experience in commodity trading, financial markets, or related trading roles
  • Strong understanding of commodity markets, supply chains, and price drivers (energy, metals, agriculture, etc.)
  • Proficiency in trading platforms, market data systems, and technical analysis tools
  • Solid knowledge of risk management, hedging strategies, leverage, and portfolio diversification
  • Excellent analytical, decision-making, and problem-solving skills
  • High level of discipline, attention to detail, and ability to perform under market pressure
  • Strong communication skills and experience working in fast-paced trading environments
